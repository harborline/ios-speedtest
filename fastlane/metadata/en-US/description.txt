Speed Test Stealth gives you a precise, no-nonsense measurement of your internet connection — download speed, upload speed, latency, and jitter — all powered by Cloudflare's global network.

WHAT IT MEASURES
• Download & Upload — tested across 14 progressive stages (100 KB → 250 MB) for accuracy at any connection tier
• Ping (latency) — unloaded round-trip time to Cloudflare's edge
• Jitter — consistency of your connection over time

HOW IT WORKS
The app uses the same measurement methodology as the Cloudflare Speed Test web app, running sequential payload stages that adapt to your connection's real capacity. Results are reported as 90th-percentile values, filtering out one-off spikes for a stable, representative number.

NETWORK AWARENESS
• Detects WiFi, 5G, 4G, 3G, and Ethernet automatically
• Identifies your carrier on cellular connections
• Shows your local IP address and approximate ISP location

HISTORY
Every test is saved locally on your device. Review past results by date, compare network types, and track how your connection changes over time.

PRIVACY
No account required. No data leaves your device except the speed-test payload traffic to Cloudflare's public endpoints. All history is stored on-device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
